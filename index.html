About Me

My name is Amir Ashtiany and I am a certified personal trainer aspiring software developer. Knowledge currently gained thruogh the George Washington University. I attended the National Personal Training Institute (NPTI) in 2015. I’ve worked for commercial gyms before, and built a strong clientele which then helped me gain the confidence I needed to start my own personal training business in 2017 within Phoenix Fitness studio. Due to COVID-19 I have been able to efficiently utilize virtual training and online programming which has become more in demand.

A few fun facts about me are that I used to play rugby, entered (and won) a few powerlifting competitions , I got engaged right before COVID-19, and I just love to lift heavy in the gym. It is very important to me to stay on top of my health due to family medical history, and to better/challenge myself every day.
