About Me

I’m a hard working and dedicated professional full stack developer interface designer with a keen eye for detail, and a determination to deliver the very highest quality. I take great pride in my work, and I always try to better myself with every project I work on. I am motivated, enthusiastic and well organised, with a keen eye for beautiful design, primarily based around user interfaces.

The main areas of my expertise are HTML(5), CSS(3), React and JavaScript (native and jQuery) but I have intermediate knowledge of MongoDB and MySQL. I have worked with a number of project’s where I have led a team and I am comfortable working under pressure to complex specifications. I regularly use version control systems (GIT) to maintain my code, and I specialise in responsive web design (RWD).
